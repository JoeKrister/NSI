Hello, you're about to enter the aquarium simulation, Trondro-Blåhaj .
This game, important to us since childhood, is a remastered aquarium simulator. 

The game is played as follows:
To earn money, left-click anywhere in the pyxel window; to buy fish, right-click in the right-hand column.
As in real life, fish are subject to the cycle of nature: they age until they die.

At bottom left, colored squares indicate the level reached. From the fourth level onwards, you'll be dressed up as one of the great predators of the animal world: Blåhaj!
Your aim will be to gobble up your little family... Only after you've had your fill will you have won.

Rousval Yann and Buchy Dreo are proud to present their version of Aqua Box, inspired by the Japanese Dan-ball game: https://dan-ball.jp/en/javagame/aquarium/

PS: the Shift key can help you fill your pockets.
